Geography

Currently Novapara does not exert any official claim to any territory at this time. For the time being territory is only regonized as territories that are regularly occupied by Novaparan citizens.

Novapara is made up of one region (Baku, mainland Novapara), one province (a Novaparan occupied territory that is not connect to the mainland region[s]) & one unincorperated territory (a territory that is under Novaparan jurisdiction but does not have a permanent population or infrastructure).

  • // B A K U
    Baku is currently the only Novaparan region and is the heart of Novapara. Located in the Salish Sea on Whidbey Island is has a warm-summer Mediterranean climate (Köppen climate classification: Csb). Unlike much of the Pacific Northwest, located centrally, Novapara doesn't recieve that haivy rain flow. It's positioned within the Olmypic Rain Shadow which buffers much of the rain.Thus the Baku only recieves annually only about 20' of rainfall vs Mukilteo (Washington State city 31 miles south) who recieves 39' annually, almost double.
    The region does have a western coast to the Salish Sea. The coastline is lined with cliffs and a rocky beach. It does support an abundant amount of sealife surpisingly. Various crabs, starfish and sea anemones can all be found at low tide.
  • // D O R E A L B A
    This province is located in east Texas and is a forty acre plot of land. It has a population of two. Dore Alba is actually one of the most sustable & developed parts of the nation, maintaining its own various resources.
    Dore Alba has a humid subtropical climate (Köppen climate classification: Cfa). The province recieves actually a bit of rain, most likely due to its position in Tornado Alley.
  • // N E W L U X E
    New Luxe is an unincorparated territory of Novapara, annexed by the Duchy of Hodiny. It is an acre streach along "Fancy Creek". Not much is known about this territory, due to the lack of any infrastructure or permanent population. It has a humid continental climate (Köppen classification: Dfa).
