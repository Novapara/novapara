Novapara

A Constitutional Island Principality

The Principality of Novapara, a Pacific Northwestern American micronation. We are a micronational design and tech center. Located on Whidbey Island we also boast a mild agracultural development, in effort to support self sustainiblity and environmenal friendly practices.

Founded January 22nd, '16, we current govern under a Constitutional Monarchy style government.
